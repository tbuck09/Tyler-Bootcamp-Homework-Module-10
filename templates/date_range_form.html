Alternative Method

The same results can be achieved by supplying a start and end date in the URL.
Example:"http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1.0/2017-07-01/2017-08-01" will query for observations made between July 01 and August 01 (inclusive) in 2017.

If no end date is provided, the end date is set to the last day with a record (August 23, 2017).
Example:"http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1.0/2017-07-01" will query for observations made between July 01 and August 23 in 2017.
